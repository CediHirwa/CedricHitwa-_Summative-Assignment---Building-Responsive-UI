    THE MANIFESTO.

    Sustainable High Performance Requires Radical Self-Awareness.

    Campus Flow harmonizes professional rigor with human well-being.

    Capacity Awareness

    Time is a finite resource. By logging sessions rather than just tasks, you account for the energy required to maintain flow without over-extension.

    Workload Intensity

    The Heat Bar provides real-time visual cues based on your daily capacity, signaling when it's time to push and when you've hit your human limit.

    Registry Integrity

    Accountability is built into the OS. Mandatory justifications for cancellations encourage reflection on why plan shifts occur.

    The Rationale

    Most planning tools view humans as "output machines." Campus Flow flips the script by focusing on energy management over task completion.

    By tracking three core pillars — Academic, Professional, and Social — we ensure that your professional development doesn't come at the cost of your human equilibrium.
